What's involved in the study

The ENHANCE study is testing whether a simple lifestyle programme can help people aged 60‚Äì80 keep their brains healthy as they get older.

The study is run by researchers at University College London (UCL), University of Exeter, Queen Mary University of London, and University of Hull, working with North London NHS Foundation Trust. It is funded by the National Institute for Health and Care Research (NIHR).

If your results suggest a few areas to work on, you'll learn which daily habits can make a difference to your brain health and get friendly coaching and simple tools to help you build new, healthy routines. These can include:

  • Being more active in ways that suit you
  • Eating a bit healthier
  • Looking after hearing and mood
  • Staying socially connected

Who can take part?

You may be able to join if you:

  • Are aged 60‚Äì80
  • Are registered with a participating GP practice, or heard about us at an event, NHS clinic, or online
